About Cyan

What is Cyan?

Cyan is a multifunctional, multi-platform bot, written in JavaScript using various JavaScript libraries. It has a variety of commands, including fun and recreational commands, utility, moderation, management commands, and much more. It is designed to be easy to use and highly functional.

What is The Cyan Empire?

The Cyan Empire is a collection of various bots, similar in code and features and available on different social media platforms, for the purposes of entertainment, utility and management.

Who made Cyan?

Cyan was created by Áà Män in 2025. It is currently maintained by Áà Män and a small team of developers.
